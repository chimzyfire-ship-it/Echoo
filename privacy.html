Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Echoo is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information in compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) of Canada.

1. Personal Data We Collect

To deliver our personalized city planning experiences, we collect and process the following categories of data:

  • Account Details: Name, email address, password.
  • Preferences: Personal interests, event tastes, budgets, and pace styles.
  • Location Information: City preference and rounded device location.
  • Allergy & Dietary Information: Optional user inputs regarding dietary limitations (e.g. nut allergies) used solely for planning recommendations.
  • Activity History: Checked-in venues and saved route details.

2. Data Security & Encryption

All sensitive health and allergy preferences you share are treated with the highest security standards. These details are stored in an encrypted database at rest, accessible only via secure system logic to protect your wellness outcomes.

3. City Intelligence Aggregate Clause

Aggregate Reporting Consent: Echoo may use anonymised, aggregate check-in and activity data to produce city intelligence reports for urban planners, real estate developers, and tourism organisations. No individual user is ever identifiable in these reports. By using Echoo, you consent to your anonymised activity data being included in aggregate reports of this kind. You can manage this choice in your profile settings at any time.
