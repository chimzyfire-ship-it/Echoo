Canada launch access

Echoo needs your location to unlock live Canadian discovery, routing, showtimes, and local date itineraries.

iPhone (iOS) Directions:
  1. Open your iPhone Settings.
  2. Tap Privacy & Security > Location Services.
  3. Scroll and tap your browser (e.g. Safari or Chrome).
  4. Select "While Using the App" and ensure Precise Location is on.
Android Directions:
  1. Open your phone Settings.
  2. Tap Apps > Chrome (or your browser app).
  3. Tap Permissions > Location.
  4. Choose "Allow only while using the app".
